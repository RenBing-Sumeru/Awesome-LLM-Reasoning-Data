# Glossary


## post-training

Training or adaptation performed after pretraining, including SFT, distillation, preference learning, reward modeling, process supervision, RLVR, and agent training.

## reasoning data

Training or evaluation records intended to teach, shape, reinforce, or audit reasoning behavior.

## verifier-bearing sample

A data item that pairs a task and model behavior with feedback from a checker, reward, judge, rubric, or environment predicate.

## feedback interface

The boundary where behavior becomes trainable or auditable through a label, checker, reward, judge, or environment.

## verification contract

The rules defining what can be checked, at what granularity, and with which failure modes.

## terminal predicate

An environment-level condition used to decide whether an agent trajectory succeeded.

## lineage

Metadata about source corpus, teacher, generator, decoding policy, verifier, filter, split, and reuse path.

## pass-rate band

A base-model- and sampling-protocol-dependent estimate of how often a prompt is solved.

## release card

A structured report describing what was released, how it was built, how it was verified, and what remains missing.
