The pipeline samples up to 500 MMLU-Pro questions per domain, separately from evaluation questions, and asks Llama-3.1-8B-Instruct for 16 step-separated chains of thought per question. It removes traces over 2,048 tokens or without a parsable answer. Llama-3.1-70B-Instruct then sees the question, reference answer, and trace, and applies explicit GOOD, OK, BAD criteria to identify the earliest bad step.

After filtering, the release contains 84,098 traces and 487,380 steps. The final model starts from LlamaPRM800K and uses LoRA for one epoch, with a classification loss only at a special token appended to each step. At use time, its step probabilities are aggregated by minimum, last, or average score for weighted majority voting, best-of-N, beam search, or Monte Carlo tree search.
