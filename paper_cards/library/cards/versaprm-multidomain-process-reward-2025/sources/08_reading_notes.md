Read the paper in the order of its dataset recipe, not its search algorithms: inspect the question split, 16-trace sampling, earliest-error label conversion, manual-label audit, and LoRA objective before interpreting the reranking curves. For reuse, validate the judge on each target domain, store the original trace and judge rationale with every label, and compare a diverse mixture with a same-size single-domain control; the paper's own ablations show that this comparison is central to its claim.
