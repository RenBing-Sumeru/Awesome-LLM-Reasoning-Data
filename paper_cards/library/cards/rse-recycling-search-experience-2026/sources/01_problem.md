Reasoning-data pipelines often discard provenance, failed candidates, or intermediate feedback, making model gains hard to attribute. Do Not Waste Your Rollouts: Recycling Search Experience for Efficient Test-Time Scaling focuses on a concrete trajectory-data decision rather than treating the training corpus as a flat set of answers.
