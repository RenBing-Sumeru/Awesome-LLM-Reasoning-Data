Use InstructDiff when the practical decision is which subset of a heterogeneous SFT pool a particular target model should consume under a fixed budget. Preserve the base checkpoint, exact warmup draw and seed, calibration hyperparameters, tokenization, NLL and entropy definitions, both percentile cutoffs, candidate identifiers, and selection iteration. Re-run the measurement with a target or same-family model rather than assuming a subset transfers, and compare against random and full-data SFT under equal compute. Its official code is helpful for reproducing the criterion, but the paper's four source datasets retain their own licenses and provenance obligations.
