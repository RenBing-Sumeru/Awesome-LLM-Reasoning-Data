Remember the chain of decisions: candidate mathematical text is paired with a continuous score, Qwen-72B supplies that score through YES/NO logits, and score bands determine the approximately 200 GB corpus used for continual pretraining. The representative Mistral-7B result is 16.14 versus 14.26 MATH accuracy for uniform sampling, but the operational lesson is not “trust one scorer.” It is to release the selection signal, hold the training budget fixed, and audit licenses and benchmark overlap before claiming that a curation rule improves learning.
