Use CROPI as a design reference when the training objective has a small, well-governed validation target and the system can store offline trajectories. It is useful for choosing an RLVR subset repeatedly, especially when domain relevance matters more than a global notion of data quality. Before reuse, decide whether the selection-validation set represents a legitimate objective rather than a leakage route, audit the offline trajectory provenance, and measure the full gradient-selection cost against the rollout savings in the target stack.
