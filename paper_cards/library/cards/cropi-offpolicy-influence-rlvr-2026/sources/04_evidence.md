The main table evaluates Qwen2.5-1.5B-Instruct, Qwen2.5-7B-Instruct, and DeepSeek-R1-Distill-Qwen-1.5B across GSM8K, MATH, Gaokao, AMC23, OlympiadBench, and AIME24. In the 1.5B setting at 1,000 steps, CROPI reports a 70.26 targeted-task average and 29.60 untargeted-task average, compared with 69.42 and 27.06 for full-data GRPO. The paper reports a 2.66-times step-level acceleration on targeted tasks while selecting 10 percent of the pool per phase.

The authors also include the cost of selection rather than reporting only optimization steps. On the 1.5B setup, each phase lists 1.2 hours for selection over 19K processed prompts and 5.2 hours for 200 training steps on eight H100 GPUs; after selection cost, the paper reports an overall 2.16-times speedup and a 2.17-times speedup to 70 percent targeted accuracy. Ablations compare random phase-wise selection and projected-gradient settings, while the evaluation excludes examples used to construct the selection-validation sets.
