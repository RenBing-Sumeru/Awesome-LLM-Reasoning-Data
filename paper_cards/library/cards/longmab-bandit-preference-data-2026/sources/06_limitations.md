The reward requires a ground-truth answer and relies on exact-match and F1 proxies, so it is not directly suitable for open-ended generation where correctness cannot be string matched. Thirty rollouts may be costly when contexts are much longer than the reported 8K–16K range, and the authors only study models mainly between 7B and 14B parameters. Wikipedia augmentation may not represent the noise, source relationships, or legal constraints of production corpora. Although code and a data download path are public, any reuse must independently check the upstream dataset, model, and synthetic-context licenses.
