This is essential for practitioners whose long-context records have reference answers and whose main bottleneck is constructing credible DPO pairs rather than inventing a new optimizer. It supplies a transparent data schema, a deterministic terminal reward, and a release with scripts for probe generation, rollouts, pair construction, DPO, and evaluation. Before adopting it, decide whether reference-answer reward, chunk size, rollout budget, and synthetic-noise process represent the intended training environment; these choices determine the distribution of both chosen and rejected responses.
