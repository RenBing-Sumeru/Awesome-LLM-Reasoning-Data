The authors remove duplicate instructions, randomly select one response from each source instance, and match two different instruction-response records to build a joint comparison dataset. GPT-3.5-Turbo-0125 labels both conditional and joint comparisons twice with reversed order; flipped labels are treated as ties. Human analysis uses qualified Mechanical Turk workers on 2,000 comparisons.

Mistral-7B is first supervised fine-tuned on instruction-response data. JPO then trains on the union of conditional and non-identical joint comparisons, whereas DPO and KTO receive the conditional data. Evaluation compares generated outputs against gold test responses for 500 unseen instructions, averaging three optimization runs and three sampling temperatures; AlpacaEval 2 gives an additional broad instruction test.
