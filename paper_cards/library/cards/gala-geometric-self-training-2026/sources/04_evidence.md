The paper compares GALA with full-data, random-sampling, and other data-selection baselines on GSM8K, MATH, MMLU-PRO mathematics, and ASDIV, using Llama-3.2-1B and Llama-3.1-8B. Its reported curves compare accuracy against training-data size for both SFT and DPO.

The authors also report ablations for subset selection, strategy prospecting, and dynamic validation, plus clarity and Self-BLEU diversity analyses. The evidence supports a data-efficiency claim, but no released curated set permits an independent record-level audit.
