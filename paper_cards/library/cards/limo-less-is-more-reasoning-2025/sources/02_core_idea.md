LIMO defines the useful training example as an olympiad-style question paired with a complete reasoning solution and answer. The 817 released records are selected for difficulty, diversity, and solution quality, so the intervention is the composition of the SFT set rather than a new reward model.

The data is intended to show a model how to organize knowledge it may already contain. It must not be read as a general-purpose math corpus: its effect depends on the base model, the selection process, and separation from evaluation problems.
