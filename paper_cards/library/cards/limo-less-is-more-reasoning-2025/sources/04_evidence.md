With 817 curated samples, the paper reports 57.1% on AIME and 94.8% on MATH, substantially above the cited earlier SFT comparisons. These numbers support the claim that selection quality can matter more than raw count in the reported setting.

They do not prove that 817 is a universal threshold or that the same recipe transfers to every model and domain. Prior knowledge in the base model, teacher style, contest provenance, and benchmark familiarity remain plausible explanations that must be tested separately.
