The key reading point is that a normalized likelihood is not neutral to how a trajectory is written. In this paper, the score's token average makes the first branch token of each step disproportionately important for short steps and diluted for long ones. The data object is therefore not just an answer trace but a trace plus a segmentation and probability aggregation rule. When auditing a reasoning-data selector, inspect whether a superficial representation feature changes the denominator of its score, then test the selected-data distribution before attributing performance gains to reasoning quality.
