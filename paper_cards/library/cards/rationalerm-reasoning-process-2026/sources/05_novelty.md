Outcome-based reward modelling normally treats an accurate final preference as the supervision target. Process reward work can add free-form explanations, but may not have a test for whether those explanations correspond to the evaluator’s actual grounds.

RationaleRM introduces an explicit, atomic, one-to-one consistency target and makes it part of the reward used for optimization. The novelty is not simply asking a model to explain itself; it is turning explanation agreement into a measurable training signal alongside the outcome.
