The central object is an answer pair with a human rationale decomposed into mutually exclusive atomic reasons, plus a model-generated rationale. The proposed MetaJudge performs strict one-to-one semantic matching between these two sets of reasons.

This yields Rationale Consistency as a process signal that cannot be inferred from a correct final choice alone. The official release includes the HelpSteer3 Human Checklist data and its atomic rationale annotations, making the matching target inspectable.
