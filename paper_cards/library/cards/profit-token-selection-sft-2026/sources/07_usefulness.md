Use ProFit when SFT has single-reference responses in a reasoning-oriented domain and token-level masking can be implemented in the training loss. Log the base model, threshold, retained-token rate, source sample, and full-token control; then compare task accuracy, instruction following, response length, and failure patterns rather than only average loss. Do not apply the low-probability rule untested to creative writing, multilingual generation, or safety-critical tasks, because the method deliberately removes expression diversity and its reported semantics assumption may fail there.
