For Llama-3-8B-Instruct, Data Whisperer using 10% of GSM8K reaches 72.46 exact match, exceeding random selection at 69.66 and the reported full-data result at 71.39. On DialogSum and BioInstruct at the same 10% budget it reaches 42.18 and 39.20 ROUGE-L, versus random 41.45 and 38.70. The paper reports that it consistently exceeds the listed selection baselines across these tasks and several subset ratios.

The cost analysis is central evidence: on Llama-3-8B, the reported selection-to-tuning ratio is 0.13–0.17 for GSM8K, compared with 1.22–1.26 for Nuggets, giving 7.41–9.38 times speedup. Attention ablations show intermediate layers are usually most stable and weak-to-strong scoring retains performance while lowering cost. These are task-specific controlled experiments, not evidence that the same score transfers to inaccessible closed models.
