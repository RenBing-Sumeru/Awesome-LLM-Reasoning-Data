The selector requires access to internal attention tensors, so it cannot directly operate on many API-only models. Its scores also depend on task-specific prompt templates and automatic metrics; a metric that poorly reflects deployment quality can select the wrong records. The reported study covers models near 3B–12B and four tasks, leaving much larger models, multilingual data, and other training objectives untested. The method often prefers lower-perplexity examples at small budgets, which may reduce rare-case coverage; upstream dataset terms and the repository implementation need separate review before reuse.
