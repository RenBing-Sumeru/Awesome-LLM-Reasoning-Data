The Card's central object is not just a selected question but a question connected to a hierarchy and to a particular student's competence profile. That distinction explains why the same 100K pool can yield different 1K mixtures for different students. The full-data failure is a useful caution: more teacher traces can harm a strong reasoning model if they are redundant or misallocated. When reproducing, first validate the attribution and per-skill measurement, then test whether explicit chains improve reasoning rather than merely making the output imitate the labels.
