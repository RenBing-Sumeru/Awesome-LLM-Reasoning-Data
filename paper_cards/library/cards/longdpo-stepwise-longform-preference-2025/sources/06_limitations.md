The authors validate outputs up to 32K length and explicitly note that current benchmarks make longer generations hard to assess. Quality rewards for creative writing have no ground truth and depend on seven designed principles plus an external LLM, so the construction rule can encode evaluator bias. The pipeline is materially more expensive than terminal DPO because it uses tree expansion, reward scoring, fact extraction, retrieval, consistency checking, and critiques; it also reports no standalone release of the generated stepwise pairs.
