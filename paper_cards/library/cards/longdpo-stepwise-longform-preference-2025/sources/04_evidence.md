On LongBench-Write-en, LongWriter-Llama with LongDPO raises the average length score from 83.12 to 87.38 and quality score from 85.10 to 88.28; its ordinary-DPO counterpart reaches 85.55 and 85.66. For outputs above 4,000 words, the reported length score rises from 80.80 to 88.25. On LongGenBench, the Llama completion rate at 32K is 68.35 with LongDPO versus 65.24 with ordinary DPO, and the Qwen model reaches 84.95 versus 82.23.

The general-task comparison does not show the same degradation seen in some ordinary-DPO results. For LongWriter-Llama, LongDPO reports TruthfulQA 40.76, MMLU 58.78, and GSM8K 61.30, above the listed base model and DPO variants. Critique ablations favor external over self-critique on the reported averages, and the memory-pool experiment improves the average LLM-AggreFact score from 72.30 without memory to 73.67 with it. Human judges also prefer LongDPO outputs on diversity, consistency, and informativeness.
