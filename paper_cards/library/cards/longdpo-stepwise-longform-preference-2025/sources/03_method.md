For 2,500 WildChat instructions, the authors run MCTS to depth four, sample four child nodes per expansion, and score each node with a seven-principle reward from Llama-3.1-70B-Instruct. Before expansion, candidate content is divided into 128-word chunks and checked against earlier factual memory using gte-Qwen2-1.5B-Instruct retrieval and an LLM consistency decision. The highest-reward consistent node is selected; a random sibling supplies the negative step.

Chosen steps with reward at most 2.5 are paired with a better sibling to obtain principle-specific external critiques. The revised step conditions on the query, prior selected steps, and up to three writing suggestions. Training applies the DPO loss to each chosen-rejected next step given its prefix, combines the collected pairs with UltraFeedback, packs sequences to 32,768 tokens, and trains 250 steps using LongWriter-Llama 3.1-8B or LongWriter-Qwen 2.5-7B-Instruct.
