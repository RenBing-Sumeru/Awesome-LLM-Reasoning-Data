For efficiency, the current implementation assigns dataset-level rather than record-level weights, so within-source quality variation is not fully modeled. It depends on a high-quality validation set; systematic validation noise can steer the learned mixture in the wrong direction. Bilevel training is also substantially heavier than ordinary SFT, with the reported three-epoch run using 16 A100-80G GPUs. The paper declares no official implementation, model, or released weighted dataset, and the licenses and provenance of all upstream sources require separate review.
