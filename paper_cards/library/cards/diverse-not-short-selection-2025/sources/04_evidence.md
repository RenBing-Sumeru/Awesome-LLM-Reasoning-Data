The paper first verifies the confound directly. Across its six text statistics, entropy and ArmoRM quality both have negative correlations with word count. After selection, the nearest diversity baseline has a chosen-minus-rejected length difference of minus 49.90 words, whereas Diverse-NS is minus 1.35 and its lightweight variant is minus 0.90. Thus the curated records preserve near length parity rather than selecting brevity as a shortcut.

With only 3,000 pairs, Diverse-NS improves diversity measurements across Divergent Associations, Persona Generation, Alternate Uses, and Creative Writing for Llama-8B, Olmo-7B, and Olmo-13B. For example, Llama-8B creative-writing DSI rises from 0.8515 to 0.8581 and four-gram diversity from 2.8550 to 2.9620. Human evaluators on creative writing give Diverse-NS win rates from 50.00% to 72.22% against the base model and from 51.52% to 72.73% against the closest baseline, depending on evaluator.
