The study uses entropy or TTR as a principal diversity signal, although neither alone captures all lexical, semantic, syntactic, and novelty dimensions. It focuses on short-story writing and one round of preference tuning, so transfer to broader tasks and repeated self-training remains unproven. Strict filtering can also leave a skew toward longer retained pairs despite within-pair parity. Finally, diversity is not a suitable sole objective where factual correctness is critical; the authors specifically caution against use for medical, legal, or scientific fact checking.
