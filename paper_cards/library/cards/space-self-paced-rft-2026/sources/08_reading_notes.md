Read SPaCe as two decisions with different time scales. Clustering and farthest-point reduction are a one-time decision about coverage; the bandit is an online decision about allocation of the next batch. Its reported small-data result does not mean any 100 examples suffice: those examples are chosen in relation to a specified pool, embedding, difficulty attribute, learner, reward, and scheduler. The limitation section is particularly important because the work has not shown that the same policy transfers to larger or non-verifiable settings.
