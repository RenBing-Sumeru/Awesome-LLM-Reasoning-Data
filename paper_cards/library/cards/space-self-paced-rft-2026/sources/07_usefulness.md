Use SPaCe when an RL or RFT budget is too small to sample a reasoning pool uniformly and when each prompt has a defensible scalar attribute such as difficulty. Preserve the pool revision, embedding checkpoint, PCA and normalization settings, cluster count, per-cluster quota and representative IDs, reward extractor, bandit prior and stagnation rule, decoding configuration, and every selected cluster over time. Compare with uniform GRPO and a static curriculum at equal rollout and update budgets. The method is most useful where one needs to explain not only which examples were retained but why their exposure frequency changed during training.
