Across six benchmarks, the paper reports that URSA-8B-PS-GRPO outperforms Gemma3-12B and GPT-4o by 8.4 and 2.7 percentage points on average.

The result supports the combined data-and-RL stack; it does not isolate whether MMathCoT scale, DualMath labels, the reward model, or PS-GRPO contributes most.
