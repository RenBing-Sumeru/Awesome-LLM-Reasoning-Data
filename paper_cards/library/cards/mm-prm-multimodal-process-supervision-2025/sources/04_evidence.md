The paper reports that 10K MM-K12 seed records yield more than 700K step-level annotations and that the PRM improves Best-of-N results on MM-K12, OlympiadBench, and MathVista. This supports the feasibility of scaling process labels from answer-level verification.

It does not prove that every generated step label is correct. Search quality, the initial policy, visual understanding, and the checker’s answer format all influence the supervision that reaches the PRM.
