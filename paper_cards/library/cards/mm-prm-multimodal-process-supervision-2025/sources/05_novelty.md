MM-PRM extends answer-verified search and process reward modeling to image-and-text mathematics, connecting seed problems, solution trees, and step labels in an automated data pipeline. It is not simply text CoT with an image attached: the terminal answer is used to govern path selection.

Search, final-answer checks, and PRMs are established ingredients. The novelty lies in combining them into scalable multimodal process supervision, while leaving visual misreading and semantically invalid but answer-correct paths as important residual risks.
