For every LongFact training question, the base policy produces 30 responses. ACPO tokenizes each response into sentences, uses SimCSE sentence embeddings with agglomerative clustering to merge semantically similar statements, and labels a cluster consistent when it contains at least two statements. A response gains one point for each fact in a consistent cluster and loses one for each fact in a non-consistent cluster.

The pipeline sorts the 30 responses by this score and records the top response as chosen and the bottom response as rejected. Those pairs train Llama-3-8B-Instruct or Phi-3-mini-4k-instruct using DPO with beta 0.1. The reported implementation trains on 2,097 LongFact questions; the paper also studies length-balanced variants and applies the same atomic-consistency selector at inference as an ablation.
