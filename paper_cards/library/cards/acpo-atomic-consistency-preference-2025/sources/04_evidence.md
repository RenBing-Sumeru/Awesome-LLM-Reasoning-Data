On LongFact and BioGen, ACPO is compared with the unaligned model and FactAlign, a supervised factuality-alignment baseline. Across Llama-3 and Phi-3 in the four reported benchmark settings, the paper reports an average factual-precision gain of 1.95 points over FactAlign. On LongFact with Llama-3-8B-Instruct, the reported F1 is 83.84 for ACPO, compared with 80.32 for FactAlign and 75.88 for the raw model.

The paper also measures construction cost. Its embedding-and-clustering route takes 3.3 seconds per example, versus 922.1 seconds for sentence facts verified by GPT-4 and 1,376.0 seconds when GPT-4 both generates and verifies atomic facts. Removing the penalty for rare facts lowers BioGen factual precision from 51.8 to 46.9, supporting the claimed role of both positive and negative consistency signals.
