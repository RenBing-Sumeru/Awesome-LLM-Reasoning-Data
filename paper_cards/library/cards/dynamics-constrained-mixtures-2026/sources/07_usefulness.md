Use DynaMiCS when a team can name both the capabilities it wants to improve and the capabilities it must not lose, and can afford small repeated evaluations on each. Store the exact target and constraint definitions, base reference scores, probe budget, update schedule, optimization penalties, and source-mixture trajectory with every run. Compare against fixed mixtures at the same total compute, report feasibility separately from target improvement, and treat an infeasible result as a failure to fine-tune rather than silently selecting a high-target but unsafe checkpoint.
