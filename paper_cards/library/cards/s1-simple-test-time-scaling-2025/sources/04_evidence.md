The paper reports that s1-32B with budget forcing exceeds o1-preview by up to 27% on MATH and AIME24, and that AIME24 rises from 50% to 57% as budget increases. This is evidence that the disclosed SFT-and-decoding combination can produce positive test-time scaling.

It does not show that 1,000 records or the string “Wait” will transfer universally. Outcomes depend on model format, end-of-thinking behavior, context capacity, teacher traces, and the task distribution.
