s1K contains 1,000 reasoning examples selected for difficulty, diversity, and quality, and supplies the supervised behavior. Budget forcing then either terminates thinking early or suppresses the ending and appends “Wait” so the model can continue and reconsider.

The core idea is to expose both sides of the recipe. The dataset is not a generic corpus, and the decoding intervention is not an annotation method; together they define a controllable test-time scaling experiment.
