The original pre-training distribution is unavailable and is replaced by RedPajama frequencies, so the coverage component is only an approximation and may vary for models with different hidden corpora. The experiments span models from 1B to 8B parameters and two training epochs, with no larger-scale test and no official implementation or ordered-data release. The authors also state that the intuitive mechanism behind the effect needs deeper analysis; the observed gains do not by themselves prove why a particular rank improves a particular model.
