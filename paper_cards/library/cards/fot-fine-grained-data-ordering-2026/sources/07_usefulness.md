This paper is useful when every example is judged worth retaining but teams need a principled way to stage exposure during fine-tuning. It supplies a record-level score, a schedule boundary, and ablations that separate temporary curriculum use from permanent sorting. Before adoption, reproduce the scoring pass on the intended base model, verify the legality and representativeness of the corpus used to approximate pre-training coverage, and measure whether the proposed order remains beneficial under the actual number of epochs and optimizer settings.
