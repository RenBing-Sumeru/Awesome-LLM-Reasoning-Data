The training record joins a web task, the current state, interaction history, two candidate thought-action continuations, a structured justification, and a winner. The justification first states reusable principles and then applies them to the particular state.

This representation makes the reward target more than a final preference label. The same record can teach a model to articulate why an action is suitable, score competing trajectories, or supply a search procedure with a preference signal grounded in visible page evidence.
