The project reports that its 7B model improves best-of-N action-selection accuracy by 9.1 points over GPT-5 on the reported web-agent benchmark. It also reports gains of up to 6.4 points when the reward model guides trajectory search against the stated comparisons.

Those results support the proposed process record and reward interface under the released benchmark and evaluation protocol. They do not establish that a generated principle remains faithful on unseen websites, altered accessibility trees, or tasks with hidden state not represented in the input.
