GenPRM recasts a PRM as a generative critic rather than a direct scalar predictor. A record links a problem, candidate solution paragraphs, Relative Progress Estimation, generated analysis, verification evidence, and a Yes/No step decision.

The release therefore supports both training and test-time aggregation. The important object is the relation between the rationale and the decision, not simply the 23K conversation count; generated text remains a claim that must be bounded by the available verification evidence.
