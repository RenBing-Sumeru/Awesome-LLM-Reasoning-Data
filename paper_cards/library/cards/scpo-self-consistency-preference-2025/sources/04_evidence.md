On MATH, two rounds of unsupervised ScPO report 19.72% greedy accuracy and 24.58% eight-way self-consistency accuracy, versus 14.46% and 18.20% for the seed model. The comparable external reward-model baseline reports 18.08% greedy accuracy after two rounds. With gold labels available, semi-supervised ScPO reaches 20.48% greedy accuracy, slightly above the 20.32% reported for gold-label IRPO.

On ZebraLogic, two rounds improve Llama-3 Instruct 8B overall puzzle accuracy from 11.6% to 18.1%. The paper's ablation shows the vote-weighted loss matters: on MATH first-iteration greedy accuracy is 17.36% with the weighted loss and 15.92% with identical pairs but unit weights. A threshold sweep also shows the trade-off: 0.5k yields 1.8K MATH pairs and 17.36%, while 0.7k yields fewer than 700 pairs and 14.76%.
