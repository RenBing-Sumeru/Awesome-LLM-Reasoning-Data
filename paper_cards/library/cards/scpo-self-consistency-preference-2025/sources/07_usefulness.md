Use ScPO when a task has extractable final answers, unlabeled but reasonably difficult prompts, and a model that can sometimes reach a majority answer. Preserve all sampled answers and vote clusters, not merely the selected pair, so later audits can measure whether consensus tracks correctness. Tune the vote threshold against held-out trusted labels when possible; if it produces too few diverse pairs or high-confidence errors, use a verifier, human labels, or a different feedback contract instead.
