Starting from seed unlabeled problems, the current model samples k responses per prompt at temperature 0.7 and top-p 0.9. For GSM8K and MATH, k is eight; final answers are clustered by exact parsed answer. The method retains a problem only if the maximum vote meets a threshold, then gives its pair a normalized vote-gap weight. It uses a stricter threshold after the first iteration as models become more consistent, and it filters newly generated problems with the same rule.

The paper trains Llama-3 Base 8B for GSM8K and MATH and Llama-3 Instruct 8B for ZebraLogic. Its reference is the model from the previous iteration. It compares unsupervised and semi-supervised ScPO with a reward-model construction baseline, self-consistency NLL training, and gold-label IRPO; two iterations are the reported default.
