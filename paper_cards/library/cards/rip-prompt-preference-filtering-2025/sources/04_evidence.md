The ICML paper reports that RIP filters 77% of training examples in its highlighted setting while improving Llama-3.1-8B-Instruct on AlpacaEval 2 length-controlled win rate by 9.4%, Arena-Hard by 8.7%, and WildBench by 9.9%.

It also reports an Arena-Hard increase from 67.5 to 82.9 for Llama-3.3-70B-Instruct, and releases a filtered WildChat dataset. These results substantiate the proposed filtering in the studied settings, but depend on reward-model scores and benchmark-specific prompt distributions.
