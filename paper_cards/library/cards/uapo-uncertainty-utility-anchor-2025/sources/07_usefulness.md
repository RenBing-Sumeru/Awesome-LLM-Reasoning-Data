This is useful for teams whose preference collection contains a large surplus of standalone judged responses or imbalanced win/lose records. It gives a way to ask whether those records can be incorporated rather than discarded, while making the uncertainty assumption explicit. Before adoption, separate actual unpaired examples from artificially broken pairs, test the anchor across sources and model sizes, compare against a simple pairing baseline, and audit whether the new objective changes behavior in the desired direction rather than merely improving a judge-based score.
