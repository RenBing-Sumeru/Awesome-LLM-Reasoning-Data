The paper does not release official code, a utility-anchor checkpoint, or a new reusable data artifact, so reproduction must reconstruct the formulation and dataset transformations. Anchor quality is tied to the source mixture and target model distribution; the authors observe that multiple datasets do not benefit every setting equally. The relationship between the learned anchor and real annotator uncertainty remains an inferred interpretation rather than a direct human calibration study, and data overlap or source-license analysis is not provided.
