WONDA asks whether the quality of formally valid loop-invariant data, rather than model scale alone, limits invariant synthesis. The primary paper is https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.15510 and the official implementation is https://github.com/idopinto/wonda. The programmatically verifiable object is a C verification query paired with a candidate invariant and the verification outcomes that establish induction and usefulness. This is not a collection of judged explanations: raw verifier output is transformed but every semantics-changing rewrite is checked again. The official paper and repository provide the data-curation recipe, verifier dependency, training setup, and reproducibility boundary needed for L4.
