Raw symbolic invariants can be formally correct but verbose, obscure, and poor teaching targets. WONDA first normalizes their AST representation, then uses an LLM to rewrite and augment candidates, and validates each transformation with a sound verification workflow. Its data object retains both provenance and acceptance: source program/query, raw output, normalized form, candidate rewrite, and verifier result. The closest baseline is direct fine-tuning on solver output; the central distinction is that readability improvements do not become labels until they pass the formal contract.
