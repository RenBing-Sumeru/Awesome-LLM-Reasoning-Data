Existing invariant datasets often preserve raw solver strings or use generation without a clear correctness boundary. WONDA's ICML 2026 contribution is to treat learnability as a curation target while retaining a programmatic verification gate after every risky rewrite. AST normalization and formal checking are established techniques; the direction signal is their integration with LLM augmentation and released training/evaluation infrastructure. It demonstrates that data quality can be optimized without downgrading the verifier contract.
