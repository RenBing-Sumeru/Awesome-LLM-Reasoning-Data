The pipeline starts from a verification query containing assumptions, a program, and a target property. It obtains raw solver/verifier invariants, normalizes them structurally, proposes semantically cleaner variants, and verifies correctness/inductiveness and sufficiency in the program-verification environment. Accepted variants become curated SFT data; rejected, timed-out, and UNKNOWN candidates must remain distinguishable. The release uses UAutomizer and runlim in its verification setup. Reproduction should pin source programs, raw invariants, rewrite-model/prompt, AST transformations, candidate count, verifier version, timeout/memory limits, and the selection rule.
