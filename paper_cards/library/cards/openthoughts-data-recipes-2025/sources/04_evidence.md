The paper reports more than 1,000 controlled experiments and reports that OpenThinker3-7B trained on OpenThoughts3-1.2M reaches 53% on AIME 2025, 51% on LiveCodeBench, and 54% on GPQA Diamond. These results support the recipe under the stated model and benchmarks.

They do not attribute every gain to data scale alone. Teacher ability, training implementation, benchmark exposure, and domain mixture can change the outcome, so the numbers must be read as evidence for a particular disclosed pipeline.
