The reported initial curation still depends on heuristic thresholds, multiple external models, and quality judgments, so the claimed efficiency excludes some selection cost and may not transfer cleanly to another corpus. The evidence is confined to mathematical reasoning, PPO-style optimization, and a small set of model scales; it does not establish that the same score works for code, dialogue, multimodal data, or different reward designs. The official paper also does not declare code or a release of the final curated and dynamic subsets, which limits direct reproduction of the exact training surface.
