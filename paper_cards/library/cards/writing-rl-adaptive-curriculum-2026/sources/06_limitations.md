The method relies on a fine-tuned judge for selection and Qwen-Plus for recurrent pairwise rewards, so judge preferences, positional bias, API availability, and cost can affect the resulting curriculum. The reported gains are strongest at 7B, 8B, and 14B writer scales and start from supervised writer models; the authors explicitly leave larger-scale experiments and the question of whether RL alone can elicit the effect from a base model for future work. The paper also does not supply a dedicated contamination analysis for its source corpora and evaluations.
