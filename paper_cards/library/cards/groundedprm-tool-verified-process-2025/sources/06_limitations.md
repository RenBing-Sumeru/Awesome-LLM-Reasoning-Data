The method's factual grounding is strongest where a mathematical tool can faithfully represent a natural-language step; it does not establish equivalent coverage for open-ended, legal, scientific, or social reasoning. MCTS, model rollouts, external tool calls, and explanation generation make data construction substantially more expensive than final-answer labeling. The paper also does not provide a broad contamination audit, and its impressive search result is tied to the stated Qwen policy, benchmarks, and tool-compatible mathematics environment.
