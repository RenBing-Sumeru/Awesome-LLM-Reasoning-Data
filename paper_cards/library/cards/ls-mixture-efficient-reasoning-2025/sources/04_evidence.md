Compared with standard SFT, LS-Mixture SFT reports an average 2.3% accuracy improvement while reducing response length by about 47.61%. This jointly measured capability and length outcome is the paper’s key evidence that the mixture does not simply trade away reasoning quality for brevity.

The result is evidence for the reported models, rewriter, and benchmark mixture. It does not prove that every short rewrite preserves a valid proof, nor that a 47.61% length reduction is desirable for tasks where detailed intermediate explanations are required for checking or user trust.
