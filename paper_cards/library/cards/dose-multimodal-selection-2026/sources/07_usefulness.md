This is essential reading for curators of visual instruction data who need an auditable selection recipe without a costly target-corpus training pass. It gives a concrete record schema—two scores plus a sampling probability—and a clear decision point before SFT. Practitioners can use it to test whether quality and alignment should be measured separately in their own data, while treating its weighted-sampling parameters, scorer prompts, and source-license checks as design choices to validate rather than defaults to copy.
