The authors could not directly test models above one billion parameters without memory-saving techniques; their 8B study is parameter-efficient rather than full-parameter training. The method is evaluated only for static offline pairs, so online sampling, changing rewards, and comparisons with PPO or GRPO remain open. Its principal alignment metric averages two LLM judges, and the favorable results at small scales do not transfer unambiguously to the constrained 8B setting.
