Fitness is explicitly noisy because GPT-4o executes the proposed rules in natural language, so a correct rule can be underscored and a fluent but wrong rule can survive. The training sources are structured inductive tasks with a clear demonstration contract; the same thresholding may not transfer to open-ended semantic reasoning. Reproduction also depends on the teacher version, sampling prompts, candidate-search budget, task splits, and the released repository remaining available.
