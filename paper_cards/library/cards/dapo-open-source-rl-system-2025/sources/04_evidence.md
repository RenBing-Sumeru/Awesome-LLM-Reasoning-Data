The paper reports 50 on AIME 2024 with Qwen2.5-32B base and releases the DAPO code and DAPO-Math-17k data. This is direct evidence that the disclosed algorithm-and-system combination can reach a competitive result under the authors’ setup.

It is not evidence that any integer-answer reward will be equally reliable or that the number transfers to other task distributions. The result remains conditional on the prompt transformation, parser, compute allocation, and benchmark protocol.
