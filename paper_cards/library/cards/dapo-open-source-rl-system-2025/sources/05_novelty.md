Earlier reasoning-RL reports often expose a final model while leaving the training recipe opaque. DAPO’s contribution is to publish the algorithm, online sampling behavior, answer-verification interface, and engineering system together, so that “how the model was trained” becomes a reviewable object.

Clipping, reward verification, and online RL are not individually new. The novelty is their integrated, open reproduction surface, which still needs separate audits for data provenance, reward exploits, and hardware-dependent behavior.
