The method acts at dataset level, so it cannot distinguish good and bad instances inside a source; the paper names instance-level mixture optimization as future work. Its immediate loss-reduction reward is a proxy for longer-term downstream value, and the chosen prior assumes original source proportions encode useful coverage rather than inherited bias. Validation is limited to two TÜLU mixtures, two LLaMA model sizes, and ten reported benchmarks. The ACL paper does not declare a dedicated official implementation or a new standalone data release, so reuse requires reconstructing the scheduler from the paper and separately respecting the upstream TÜLU collection's mixed source restrictions.
