Use this work when a team needs a judge that will later score, filter, or create training records across more than one evaluation format. It provides an auditable way to turn judgment annotations into three complementary DPO consumers and to test which task types are necessary. Before applying it, check rubric compatibility across sources, sample enough teacher candidates to obtain both positive and negative outputs, preserve label provenance, and separately evaluate the judge in the downstream data-construction role rather than assuming benchmark judge accuracy guarantees good reward data.
