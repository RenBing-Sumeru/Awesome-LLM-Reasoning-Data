The authors explicitly limit evidence to 7B and 8B models and English tasks, leaving larger-scale and multilingual behavior unverified. The end points and shape of the schedule remain design choices, even though the reported cosine setting is robustly better than several fixed values in the tested setup. Because the method reweights rather than diagnoses records, it cannot tell whether an early-suppressed pair is mislabeled, genuinely difficult, or useful for an unmeasured capability; benchmark gains also rely on LLM judges for the two primary alignment evaluations.
