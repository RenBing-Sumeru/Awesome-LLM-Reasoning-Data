With cosine scheduling, the Llama 3 result reaches 56.4% length-controlled win rate on AlpacaEval 2.0 and 36.0% win rate on Arena-Hard, compared with 55.2% and 35.0% for the best tuned fixed FocalPO setting. For Mistral, it reports 24.1% length-controlled AlpacaEval win rate and 18.2% Arena-Hard win rate, above the best fixed counterpart at 22.9% and 17.3%. Main results are averaged over three seeds and the marked improvements over the best FocalPO setting are tested with paired t-tests.

The proposed training dynamic is also measured directly: the share of gradient contribution from correctly ranked pairs falls from 78% early in training to 47% late, close to DPO's roughly 46%, whereas fixed FocalPO remains near 61%. On five OpenLLM tasks, the Llama configuration has the best reported average, 66.4%, though GSM8K is 1.6 points below DPO and the paper says this difference is not statistically significant. These observations support a staged weighting interpretation, not a claim that every capability rises.
