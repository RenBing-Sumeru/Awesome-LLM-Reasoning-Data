The evidence is limited to 1.5B, 3B, and 7B models and mainly to GSM8K and DAPO-MATH-17K, with one code benchmark. The scoring procedure still requires a warm-up model, multiple rollouts, gradients, random projection, and pairwise comparison, so its practical cost and approximation quality need reassessment at much larger scale. Success rate assumes a reliable binary verifier and can be unstable when only a small number of rollouts is available. The warm-up subset choice changes results, and no official code or released selected dataset is declared.
