Remember M2PO as a candidate-set method: it first changes how translation candidates are labeled and ranked, then changes how the ranked set becomes an optimization loss. The central empirical signal is not only the WMT23 score but also the human evaluation and ablations showing that the faithfulness filter and multi-pair contrasts matter. Its strongest lesson for reuse is to record the whole candidate set and the scoring lineage, because a single chosen-rejected pair cannot reveal which partial errors were filtered, retained, or emphasized.
