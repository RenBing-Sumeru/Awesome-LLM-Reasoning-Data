M2PO relies on proxy scores and a semantic-alignment classifier to decide what is faithful, so their blind spots can become systematic training errors. The main construction also uses GPT-4o-mini candidates, making exact reproduction dependent on a proprietary service and its changing behavior, even though the paper reports an open-source variant. The evidence is concentrated on six translation directions and selected MT benchmarks; before applying the approach to another domain, audit the label distribution, candidate cost, language coverage, and whether the multi-pair objective improves the target metric rather than only the internal proxy.
