Use this paper when a training pipeline already generates several plausible outputs per input and a best-worst pair conceals useful middle cases. Preserve the source, every candidate, each quality and faithfulness score, confidence value, fused rank, pair weight, and the candidate-generation version; otherwise the preference decision cannot be audited or replayed. For translation, the paper is especially useful where omissions and hallucinations matter more than fluent phrasing, but its full recipe should be adapted only after validating the external scoring contract on the intended language pair.
