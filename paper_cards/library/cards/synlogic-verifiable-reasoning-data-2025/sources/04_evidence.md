The release describes about 16K easy and 33K hard training instances across 35 families. The paper reports that a 32B SynLogic-trained model exceeds DeepSeek-R1-Distill-Qwen-32B by 6 points on BBEH, supporting the usefulness of the stated synthetic-verification recipe.

The result does not establish that the synthetic distribution represents all natural reasoning. It may reflect adaptation to the task templates and their answer formats, so transfer requires independent tests beyond the generator’s own families.
