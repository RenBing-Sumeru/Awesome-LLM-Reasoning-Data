Use this paper as the experimental template for a large SFT curation pipeline. For a proposed selector, compare repeated random subsets, diversity, length, and the proposed rule under identical data budgets and training schedules; report distributions and significance, not only the best run. It is particularly useful before funding a costly scoring service. Do not use the paper to declare that one subset is intrinsically “high quality,” because its result concerns training outcomes under specific models and tasks.
