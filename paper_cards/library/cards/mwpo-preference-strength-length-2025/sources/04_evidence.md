In the Mistral-7B-Base setting, MWPO reports a 23.8% length-controlled win rate on AlpacaEval 2, versus 15.1% for DPO, while average output length falls from 1,719 to 1,557 tokens. It also exceeds the reported SimPO and WPO length-controlled win rates of 20.8% and 23.5%. Across GSM8K, ARC-Challenge, TruthfulQA, MMLU, and GPQA, its average exact-match score is 46.16, compared with 44.90 for DPO.

The ablations identify a real trade-off rather than a single free gain. Reward-margin weighting alone improves the reported length-controlled win rate but makes answers longer; length weighting alone shortens answers by more than 20% relative to DPO under the shown settings, but excessive control removes needed content. The mixed objective is the evidence that the two record-level signals can balance each other in the reported setup.
