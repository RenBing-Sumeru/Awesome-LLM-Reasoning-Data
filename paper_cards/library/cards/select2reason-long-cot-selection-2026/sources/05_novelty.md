Previous curation commonly scores examples by a broad quality estimate, diversity target, or raw length. Those choices do not explicitly connect a question's value to the behavior of the model that will be trained on it.

Select2Reason jointly ranks model-relative difficulty and non-redundant trace length. Its novelty is the explicit selection target linking a question to the reasoning behavior attached to it, and the use of that target to decide which records enter supervised fine-tuning rather than merely describing the pool.
