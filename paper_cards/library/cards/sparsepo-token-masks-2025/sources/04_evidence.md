On Anthropic HH single-turn dialogue, the common sparse-mask configuration wins against DPO at every reported temperature, with a 62.96 percent win rate at temperature zero versus 44.32 for TDPO v1. The authors report an average gain of 19.56 percentage points over that baseline. On Open LLM Leaderboard reasoning tasks with Pythia-1.4B, the dense configuration reaches a 40.80 average, above DPO at 39.65 and the listed SePO baseline at 36.56, while the common sparse configuration reaches 40.69.

For TL;DR summarization, SparsePO variants also beat DPO responses in the pairwise study; the independent-mask variant reaches 55.20 percent at temperature zero, while the dense variant is preferred at the other reported temperatures. The discussion reports an average 17.2 percentage-point gain over TDPO v1 in that comparison and finds comparable relevance, diversity, and faithfulness measurements for several configurations. The evidence is therefore multi-domain, but it also shows that the best mask choice is task dependent.
