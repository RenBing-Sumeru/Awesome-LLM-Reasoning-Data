The synthetic-pair accuracy evidence relies on GPT-4 judgments of 200 samples, and the method still needs a principle generator whose quality can affect the data. Improvements are smaller for already aligned models and not uniform across objective benchmarks. The reported data come mainly from UltraFeedback and English alignment tasks, so transfer to other languages, domains, and safety policies is unproven. No official code, model, or generated dataset is declared, and the licenses of the prompts, models, generator output, and external judge must be checked before reuse.
