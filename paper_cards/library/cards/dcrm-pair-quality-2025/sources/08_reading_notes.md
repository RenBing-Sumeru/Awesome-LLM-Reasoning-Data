Remember the paper as a claim about contrast quality, not simply about preference labels: the chosen and rejected answers should differ in what the model is meant to learn, not mainly in stylistic or distributional noise. The key formula combines reward margin with token edit distance and reference-policy probability difference, and the practical procedure reselects the best pair from an existing response pool. The 0.59 reported correlation and the Best-of-N-squared results are useful evidence, but both remain conditional on the reward model, candidate generators, and conversational evaluations used in the study.
