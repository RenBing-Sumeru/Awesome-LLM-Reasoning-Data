Use DCRM when an offline preference pipeline already has multiple candidate responses per prompt and the question is which pair to send to DPO or a related objective. Record the reward-model version, reference-policy version, response-generation setup, the three raw terms, and the candidate-pool size; these are part of the selection decision rather than incidental metadata. The paper is especially useful for diagnosing why a high-margin pair may train poorly, but it is not a substitute for checking whether the preference label itself expresses the behavior the downstream model should learn.
