Across two datasets and four model families, the paper reports that high-validation-loss examples reduce alignment performance. The reported capacity study finds the preferred retained fraction rising from 64% for a 3B Qwen model to 81% for a 14B model.

Selective DPO is reported to improve AlpacaEval 2 win rate by 9–16% over DPO and to outperform several objective-level variants in the tested settings. The result supports capacity-aware selection, but its validation-loss proxy and threshold require rechecking for another model or dataset.
