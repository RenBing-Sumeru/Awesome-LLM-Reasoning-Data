Existing process reward models are usually textual and commonly return a score for a step. Multimodal answer checking can instead score only the final response, leaving no representation of whether an intermediate claim agrees with the diagram.

GM-PRM adds image alignment, step purpose, logical validity, and a correction target to the process record. Its novelty is this multimodal corrective supervision object and its use in selection, not simply applying a vision-language model to a math answer.
