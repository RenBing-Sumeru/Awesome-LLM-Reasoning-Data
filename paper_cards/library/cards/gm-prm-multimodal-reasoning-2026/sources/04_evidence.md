The paper evaluates the model on multimodal mathematical reasoning and reports that its process feedback improves the stated selection procedure over the compared reward and selection baselines. The data release makes the record format and its 19,614-item scale available for inspection.

The evidence is specific to the paper’s image sources, generated labels, and mathematical benchmarks. It does not show that generated corrections are formally valid, or that the model can assess a diagram when the required visual detail is absent from the referenced image.
