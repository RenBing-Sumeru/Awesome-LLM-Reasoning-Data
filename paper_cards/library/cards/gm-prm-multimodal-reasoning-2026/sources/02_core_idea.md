Each training item contains a multimodal problem, a stepwise solution, and step-level judgements of the step purpose, image alignment, and reasoning logic. For an incorrect step, the record also identifies the first error and a corrected continuation.

This makes the reward model generative rather than purely scalar: it can explain a process defect and propose a repair. The official GM-PRM-20K release contains 19,614 such records, with images referenced from the underlying multimodal source.
