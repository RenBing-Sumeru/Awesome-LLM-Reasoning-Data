Use VRPO when RL already relies on weak verifiers, majority-vote pseudo-labels, or learned reward models and training exhibits unstable advantages or reward hacking. Log reward provenance, noise construction, value-prediction error, explained variance, selected token fraction, response length, and task result together; otherwise an apparent performance gain cannot be separated from a change in reward exploitation. It is a fit for improving the consumption of noisy supervision, not a replacement for repairing a fundamentally invalid reward specification.
