VRPO does not resolve all kinds of reward noise, and its value-side regularization adds architecture, threshold, and auxiliary-loss choices that must be tuned. The rule-based experiments are confined to mathematics and science, with single-dataset training and evaluation per model, while the dialogue data are not released as a standalone artifact. The method can also inherit bias from the frozen language model and from the reward source. A reuse should test whether its token selection suppresses important minority reasoning, how sensitive results are to the bottleneck and activation fraction, and whether gains persist under independently audited rewards.
