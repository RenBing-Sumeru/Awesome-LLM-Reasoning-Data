The essential move is to make “what the model should practice” an internal, testable property rather than a generic quality score. Keep the three-way split explicit: feature-identification examples, selectable training records, and final evaluation data have different roles. The causal filter is what separates the method from frequency-only activation ranking. The strongest reported case is 50% math-data selection for Gemma-2-2B, so do not generalize the 17.4% figure to every task. Treat the absence of a dedicated artifact release as a reproducibility risk.
