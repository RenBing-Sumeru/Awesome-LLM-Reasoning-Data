Use IGDS when an SFT project has a large pool, a measurable target task, and access to model activations or suitable SAEs. Build a small held-out feature-validation set, identify and intervene on candidate features, score the pool, and compare the selected subset with random and full-data SFT under the same budget. Report the model/SAE pair, layer, position, score threshold, upstream licenses, and test split; do not use it as evidence that every highly activated example is correct, or when causal feature validation cannot be run.
