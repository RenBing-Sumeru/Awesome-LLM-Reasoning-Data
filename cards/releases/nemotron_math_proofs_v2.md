## TL;DR

NVIDIA releases AoPS-derived proof, verification, and meta-verification conversations generated by DeepSeek-V4-Pro.

## 1. What is this work?

It replaces only the Cascade-2 natural-language proof subset, not its proof-refinement data.

## 2. What data object does it expose?

Two-turn messages with problem, subset, UUID, and proof/verification/meta-verification content.

## 3. What is the verifier / reward / judge / environment?

Verification is natural-language teacher judgment with embedded boxed 0/0.5/1 signals; no formal checker is exposed.

## 4. How is the data constructed?

AoPS-derived v1 problems receive DeepSeek-V4-Pro generation and DeepSeekMath-V2-style verification prompts.

## 5. How can it enter post-training?

It can support proof SFT and evaluation, but embedded scores need parsing and independent quality validation.

## 6. What should readers audit?

Pinned revision, full JSONL count, score distribution, AoPS provenance, and teacher configuration.

## 7. What is missing or risky?

Dataset Server is partial relative to card totals; filtering, failures, and independent correctness evidence are unknown.

## 8. Why it matters for post-training reasoning data

It makes self-verification traces reusable while showing why natural-language verification is not a formal proof contract.
